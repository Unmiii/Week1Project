About Me

Hi friends, I'm Michelle and I am a front-end web developer dedicated to creating user-friendly and interactive web pages.

What drew me to web development was the satisfaction of constantly creating something new and the solving of puzzle-like issues that constantly come up in coding.

Since my first tinkering with code at HackerYou in 2015, I have created accessible and responsive websites with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, and WordPress. I believe that a website can and should be both functional and beautiful to enhance the user's experience.
